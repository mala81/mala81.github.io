Powerful Features for Every Runner

1

Pace Calculator

Easily calculate and convert between:

  • Distance (km and miles)
  • Pace (min/km and min/mi)
  • Speed (km/h and mi/hour)
  • Calculate any value (distance, time or pace) based on the other two

Set any value and it will instantly convert to the other unit. Set any two values, and the third will be calculated automatically. The first value is locked, but you can continue adjusting the others â€” the calculation will update accordingly. You can also lock a different value, use predefined distances, or create your own.

2

Pace Band

Create personalized pace bands for races and training runs:

  • Set any distance and target time
  • Customize split lengths
  • Use predefined distances/splits or define your own
  • Print the band to use during your race
3

Split Calculator

Track and analyze your running splits:

  • Add splits manually or generate them automatically (import from Strava coming)
  • Calculate using distance & time, speed & time, or pace & time
  • Select splits to view their combined stats
  • Perfect after treadmill sessions or interval training to get the total distance run or to calculate the average pace for intervals
4

Cheat Sheet

Create custom pace and speed conversion charts:

  • Perfect for quick reference during workouts
  • Know what pace corresponds to different speeds
  • Add multiple charts for different pace ranges
  • Access your cheat sheets anytime, anywhere
